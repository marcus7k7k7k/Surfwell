Our Story

IN THE CARIBBEAN
Surfwell provides a luxury surf vacation in the Caribbean with high-quality boutique accommodation, tasty & healthy food, and a unique friendly social vibe between staff and guests, located in the tropical surf town of Cabarete, on the North Coast of the DR.

PROFESSIONAL SURF INSTRUCTION
Our in-house surf instructors have years of experience teaching beginners and intermediate surfers how to progress in a fun, safe and quick manner. We are blessed with year-round surf, so anytime is a good time to join us.

Qualified Coaches

At Surfwell surf camp, all our surf lessons are given in groups in a structured manner, using properly I.S.A. qualified surf coaches.

This means that Surfwell's surf instructors have all completed specific training in teaching techniques, including Practical and Theory Lessons, Life Guard Training and CPR/Basic First Aid.

As a result, you could not get better surf lessons on your learn to surf holiday.

FAQ

I am new to surfing, is this right for me?

Teaching people how to surf is what we specialize in here. We set out to be the best surfing store for beginners. We have qualified surf coaching instructors who can teach the sport of surfing in a safe, fun and quick manner.

I've heard that surfing can be dangerous. How could you ensure safety for beginners?

While surfing can indeed pose risks, especially for those who are inexperienced or in challenging conditions, our teaching spot is carefully chosen for beginner surfers. We prioritize safety above all else and take measures to ensure there are no dangerous rips, currents, or sea creatures in the area. Our qualified instructors also provide thorough safety instructions and are present in the water to supervise and assist beginners throughout their lessons.

Do I need to be a strong swimmer to try surfing?

While basic swimming skills are recommended for safety reasons, you don't need to be an expert swimmer to start surfing. Our instructors provide guidance on water safety and will be nearby to assist you if needed.

I've never tried surfing before. What do I need to prepare in advance?

You don't need any prior knowledge or skills to try surfing for the first time. Our instructors will provide all the necessary equipment and guidance to help you enjoy the fun of surfing effortlessly.

I'm worried that I won't be able to master surfing skills. What kind of support do you offer for beginners?

Our coaching team has designed courses specifically for beginners. They will teach you basic surfing skills using professional teaching methods and provide you with full support and encouragement throughout the process

I have some physical limitations, such as a waist injury. Am I suitable for trying surfing?

The physical requirements for surfing vary from person to person, but generally, good health is not a barrier to participating in surfing courses. We recommend that you inform us of your specific needs in advance so that we can provide appropriate support and advice tailored to your situation.
